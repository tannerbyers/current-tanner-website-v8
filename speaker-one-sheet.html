About Tanner

Tanner Byers is a full-stack software engineer and AWS expert who helps teams build faster and smarter. With nearly a decade of experience building at AWS, healthcare tech, and startups, Tanner brings practical insights from the trenches of production systems.

At AWS Professional Services (2022-2024), Tanner developed a React/TypeScript micro-frontend library that saved 100+ developer weeks across 13 teams, built document repositories that cut search time from 90 seconds to 15 seconds, and designed CloudFormation and IAM policies for AWS JAMs.

His talks are technical enough for senior developers and clear enough for PMs who pretend to understand YAML.

Talk Topics

AI in the Dev Workflow (That Actually Works)

How real engineers use AI tools without losing code quality or their jobs. This talk covers practical integration of AI assistants into developer workflows, addressing quality concerns, productivity gains, and where AI helps (and where it doesn't).

Serverless Gone Wild

What AWS Lambda, EventBridge, and DynamoDB can teach you about building lean systems that scale. Learn architectural patterns, cost optimization strategies, and lessons from production serverless deployments.

From Scripts to Systems

Lessons from automating AWS deployments before it was cool (and before it worked). This talk covers the evolution from manual processes to infrastructure-as-code, CI/CD pipelines, and building reliable automation.

How to Think Like a CTO Before You Are One

What startups miss about technical debt, prioritization, and people. Practical advice for engineers who want to level up their strategic thinking around architecture, team dynamics, and shipping products.
